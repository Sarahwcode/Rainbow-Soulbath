Rainbow Soulbath

"Sound therapy is increasingly recognised for its ability to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and ease anxiety. However, through the unique way I deliver my sessions, I've observed that they go deeper than just rejuvenation. I open doors for you to have your own experience. My sessions guide you into a present, open-hearted space where you feel safe to explore your inner world. This environment encourages authentic self-expression and a deeper connection to your true self, without fear of judgment. In this way, I create a supportive space where profound healing and personal growth can unfold."

Women's circle

Embark on a journey of inner discovery within our Women's Circle. Our sound bath experience, paired with the gentle guidance of a cacao ceremony, will help you connect with your deepest self. Allow the vibrations to awaken your intuition and guide you towards a deeper understanding of your heart.

Vibrant Healing & Exploration

Join us for a group session! We'll use ancient wisdom from Indigenous cultures to help you discover exactly what you need. We'll work with sound and vibrations to guide you on your journey. Experience a sense of peace, release stress, and connect with your inner wisdom.

Transformative Experience

This is a transformative experience where sound and frequencies resonate with the soul, mind, and body. In our Sound Symphony, we utilise two sets of crystal bowls, a crystal xylophone, shamanic drumming, and hand-selected songs to guide you through your journey. We invite you to arrive with an open heart and mind, allowing the sound and vibrations to navigate what you need.

The Power of Sound

Then, I'll use crystal bowls and other instruments to guide you into a state of deep relaxation. You'll leave feeling grounded, clear-headed, and refreshed.
