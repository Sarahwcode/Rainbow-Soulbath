Rainbow Soulbath

"Sound therapy is increasingly recognized for its ability to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and ease anxiety. However, through the unique way I deliver my sessions, I’ve observed something even deeper: participants are guided into a present, open-hearted space where they feel safe to engage in heartfelt conversations. This environment encourages authentic expression—speaking from the heart and receiving with love—without fear of being triggered. In this way, I create a supportive space where meaningful connections and healing can unfold."
